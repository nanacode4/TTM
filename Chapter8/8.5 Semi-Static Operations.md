
## 8.5 Semi-Static Operations (SSO)

**General**

SSO are where the workforce and plant are required to either move continuously along a road or make frequent short duration stops to carry out work SSO（单次服务作业）是指工人和设备需要沿道路持续移动或频繁进行短时停车以开展工作的区域。

Level 1& 2 **only** / 15 minute.

**Vehicles and Equipment**

**Planning Semi-Static Operations**

**TTM for Semi-Static Operations**

WK 001 Roadworks Ahead, P 002 Length（within 1km，never more than 2km

the maximum length of the works site shall be 10km. 

WK 001 Roadworks Ahead sign + P 002 shall be placed every 2km. 

P 082 /Additional signage may be repeated at 500m intervals.
